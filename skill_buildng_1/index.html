Team Manager - Joani

Joani will be responsible to effectively communicate with the client and the team. She will manage workflow, address and solve any problems that may arise, and do her best to meet the needs of the team. Joani’s primary focus is project completion that results in a satisfied client and a team that is proud of their work.

Lead Developer - Chris

The lead developer will be focused on the functionality of the site. They are responsible for installing and configuring WordPress plugins, installing and configuring the WordPress theme that the designers choose, creating child themes to edit the main theme. The lead developer will also be responsible for any configurations of the web server or file transfers to the web server. Because we are building a WordPresss site, it's unlikely that we have to write much code. If we do need to write any code, it would be the job of the lead developer.

A purpose statement and team objectives for the project.

“Team Couve Living” is comprised of five talented students in Clark Colleges CGT 206 Web Design class. Our primary focus is to create and develop a WordPress website for our client. Our aim is to work together cohesively as a team, demonstrating excellence in our ability to meet and exceed course requirements. By the end of the quarter our aim is to present to our client with a high-quality website that reflects our skills, both individually and as a team.

How your meetings will be structured.

Team Meeting will be held at the beginning if each class. Our meetings are structured as followed: Joani will provide an outline of each meeting’s agenda. “Team member” will take notes and distribute them via Slack.

Tuesday: Planning Meeting

  • Each team member will give a status update on their tasks.
  • Discuss what has gone well and what needs improvement.
  • Review upcoming project due dates.
  • Agree upon task assignments for the week.

Thursday: Stand-up meeting

  • Each team member will give a status update on their tasks.
  • Discuss what has gone well and what needs improvement.
  • Evaluate and adjust task assignment if needed.

How communication will occur and be managed.

Our agreed upon mode of communication is through Slack. Each team member will respond to each other in a timely manner in keeping with our tasks and deadlines.

The decision making process

Our team will engage in equitable discussions and vote on items that have differing opinions.
