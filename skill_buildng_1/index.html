Meeting Structure

How our meetings are structured

Team Meeting will be held at the beginning of each class. Our meetings are structured as followed:

Joani will provide an outline of each meeting’s agenda

“Team member” will take notes and distribute them via Slack.

Tuesday: Planning Meeting

  1. Each team member will give a status update on their tasks.
  2. Discuss what has gone well and what needs improvement.
  3. Review upcoming project due dates.
  4. Agree upon task assignments for the week.

Thursday: Stand-up meeting

  1. Each team member will give a status update on their tasks.
  2. Discuss what has gone well and what needs improvement.
  3. Evaluate and adjust task assignment if needed.
